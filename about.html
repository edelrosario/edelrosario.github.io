About Me

My name is Elaine Del Rosario, and I am currently a 4th year Digital Arts and Sciences student at the University of Florida. While I do have experience in both programming and art, I am geared towards the more artistic side of my major. My dream is to become a concept or character artist for either an animation or video game company.

Industry & Research Experiences

In the past year, I was an intern of a kickstarter game company located in Gainesville called Immersed Games. There, I was able to learn how concept artists operate in a game company as well as enhance my skills in digital art. I have also taken a course here at the University of Florida where I collaborated with a team to produce a game from start to finish.

Hobbies & Activities

A couple of my notable hobbies are playing video games and drawing, either traditional or digital art. Some of games I play are primarily on the XBOX 360, PS2, Nintendo 3DS, or PC. My favorite game series are the Pokemon and Final Fantasy franchises.

On campus, I am currently the president and formerly an external vice president of an Asian sub-organization called the Korean Undergraduate Student Association, also known as KUSA. In the organization, my board and I strive to spread awareness of the Korean culture on the UF campus through cultural shows and general body meetings. As a former external vice president, I kept in contact with the other Asian American sub-organizations both at UF and universities throughout Florida about any events that were going on.
